Chicago Public Health Story – Low Birth Weight

The map above highlights how the Low Birth Weight indicator varies across Chicago’s 77 community areas.

The citywide average is 10.10, but some communities face significantly higher challenges.

The highest value is found in Avalon Park, reaching 19.70. This suggests potential disparities in prenatal care, maternal health services, and overall socio-economic conditions.

The bar charts and histogram provide additional context, showing which neighborhoods are most affected and how this indicator is distributed across the city.
